## FAQ
**Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?**  
Yes, your plan is billed monthly and can be changed at any time. Prorated charges will apply.

**What happens if I exceed my AI prompts on the free plan?**  
You will still be able to deploy your code, but the AI editor features will be paused until the next billing cycle.

**Do you offer open-source discounts?**  
Yes! We offer free Pro accounts for verified maintainers of open-source projects. Contact support to apply.
