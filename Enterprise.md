# CogniCode for Enterprise

Scale your engineering operations securely. CogniCode Enterprise is designed for large teams that require strict compliance, dedicated resources, and advanced AI customizations.

## 1. Advanced Security & Compliance
- **Custom Vulnerability Scanning**: While standard users get OWASP top 10 protection, Enterprise users can define custom AST parsing rules specific to their internal security policies.
- **SOC2 & HIPAA Compliance**: Deployed on isolated infrastructure ensuring maximum data privacy.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Granular permissions for developers, devops engineers, and project managers.
- **SSO Integration**: SAML, Okta, Google Workspace, and Microsoft Entra ID support for secure authentication.

## 2. Dedicated Infrastructure
- **Private Cloud or On-Premise**: Deploy the CogniCode platform entirely within your own VPC or on-premise servers.
- **Dedicated CI/CD Runners**: Never wait in a queue. Get dedicated, high-compute build servers for lightning-fast Docker deployments.
- **Isolated Databases**: Your code, logs, and AI context memory are stored in completely isolated, encrypted database clusters.

## 3. Custom AI Models & Deep Memory
- **Bring Your Own Model (BYOM)**: Integrate your own fine-tuned LLMs or enterprise OpenRouter accounts instead of our default models.
- **Deep Project Memory**: The AI processes your *entire* enterprise codebase, creating a centralized knowledge graph of your company's microservices, APIs, and architectural decisions spanning years.

## 4. Advanced DevOps Control
- **Multi-Cloud Deployments**: Deploy not just to CogniCode's managed cloud, but directly to your own AWS, GCP, or Azure clusters.
- **Advanced Telemetry**: Export logs directly to Datadog, Splunk, or New Relic.
- **Automated Disaster Recovery**: Geo-redundant backups of all environments and rollback states.

## 5. White-Glove Support
- **Dedicated Success Manager**: A dedicated technical account manager to assist with onboarding and architectural planning.
- **24/7 Priority Support**: 99.99% Uptime SLA and less than 1-hour response time for critical incidents.
- **Custom Integration Engineering**: Need CogniCode to talk to a legacy internal tool? Our team will build it for you.
